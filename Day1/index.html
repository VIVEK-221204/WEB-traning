Nancy Jewel McDonie (born 13 April 2000), known professionally as Nancy (Korean: 낸시), is a South Korean and American singer, actress, and host.
She is a former member of the South Korean girl group Momoland, which was formed on November 10, 2016, through the Mnet's reality survival show Finding Momoland. In 2023, she also began performing in live musical theater.
